About us

Established in 2017, YKP-FM is currently one of the largest on the Andaman Sea in Thailand. Serving vessels from Myanmar to Singapore, YKP FM’s core business is the development and operation of a shipyard, focusing on the repair and maintenance of vessels as well as building new vessels. With a dedicated and capable team, we have successfully completed over 55 projects of servicing maritime vessels.

Located at a strategic land in Kantang, Trang Province, Thailand, its latest expansion brings an upgraded slipway and airbag zone, a new travel lift zone and a boat ramp zone. Sporting one of the largest slipways, it can accommodate of up to a capacity of 3,000Mt vessel. We have ventured into various markets, such as Fishery & Aquamarine, Offshore Oil & Gas, Ferry & Passenger, Military & Defence, Towing Tugs, etc. Currently in the pipelines our new travel lift zone and boat ramp zone, will enable us to venture into the premium markets of pleasure craft & yacht.

At YKP-FM we strive to be the leading provider of quality mantainence and repair services to the marine industry while upholding the standards and satisfaction of our existing customers.

Repair & Maintenance

We specialize in every kind of marine construction, fabrications, conversions, refits, and repairs for all types of commercial and government vessels.

Fully Lincesed Shipyard

Being the only fully license shipyard in Trang, we are permitted to all shipbuilding, ship repair, ship breaking and boat building, making us a full service shipyard.

Skilled Labour

Our technicians possess a good grasp of mechanical knowledge of marine electrical systems and electronics. They are also skilled with marine carpentry, fiberglass, paint and varnish.

Large Vessel Docking

We have an area size of 57,595 meters square/ 1395 boat- meters which allows for large vessels docking.

Specialized Docking Zones

We also have various docking options which can be sectioned into 4 specialized zones : Slipway zone, Airbag zone, Travel lift zone and Boat ramp zone.

Strategic Location

We also own the advantage of being geographically ideal that is in Trang, Thailand, in the middle of the Andaman Sea which allows us to serve the region ranging from Myanmar to Singapore.
